About This System

Designed for FUOYE Community

This student complaints and feedback portal is specifically built for Federal University Oye-Ekiti (FUOYE) to provide a transparent, efficient, and user-friendly platform for managing student concerns, suggestions, and feedback.

  • Transparent complaint handling process
  • Multiple user roles and permissions
  • Professional analytics and reporting
  • Mobile-responsive design
  • Secure data management

  • View complaint history
  • Receive notifications
